About the Contest App

Welcome to our Contest App! This platform is designed to provide a comprehensive system where users can participate in various contests and track their progress. Our goal is to create a user-friendly and engaging environment for competitive activities.

This app was developed by Dimitar and Desislava, who are dedicated to building high-quality web applications. We hope you find this platform useful and enjoyable.

App Features

  • User registration and authentication
  • Contest creation and participation
  • Leaderboard and ranking system
  • User profiles and badges
  • Responsive design
